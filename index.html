Roshi

The go to recepie for Roshi by shareefa

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 table spoon oil
  • 2/3 tea spoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cup boiling water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the ingredients in a bowl for about 3 to 4 mins.
  2. Make small balls from the mixed dough.
  3. Flatten the balls into thin circular shapes with a rolling pin.
  4. Heat a flattop at max heat!
  5. Heat up the roshi on the flat top, 3 seconds each side.
  6. Enjoy the roshi!
